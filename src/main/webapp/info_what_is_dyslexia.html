What is Dyslexia?

Dyslexia is a neurodevelopmental learning difference that primarily affects accurate and fluent word reading, spelling and decoding. It is not related to intelligence or effort.

  • Commonly involves difficulty mapping letters to sounds.
  • May cause slow reading, inconsistent spelling, and high fatigue while reading/writing.
  • Early support significantly improves learning outcomes.
Important: Screening tools support early detection but do not replace formal clinical diagnosis.
