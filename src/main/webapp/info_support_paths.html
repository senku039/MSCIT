Support Paths

  • Structured literacy / multisensory phonics instruction.
  • Classroom accommodations (extra time, reduced copying load, clear worksheets).
  • Assistive tools: text-to-speech, spell support, reading overlays.
  • Home support: short daily guided reading and positive reinforcement.
  • Regular progress monitoring with teacher-specialist collaboration.
Best outcomes: Early, consistent and individualized support plans.
